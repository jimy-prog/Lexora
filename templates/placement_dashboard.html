Tablet URL Address

Open the following URL on your iPad mini or tablet browser:

https://teacher-admin.onrender.com/placement/take
  1. Click New Placement Test, choose a target level, and enter the client's name.
  2. Write down or show the Access PIN (e.g. 5829) on the screen.
  3. Enter the PIN on your iPad / tablet to unlock the quiz for the client.
  4. The test will automatically score itself and appear in the results ledger below.
